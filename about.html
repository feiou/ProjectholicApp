About

An independent studio building thoughtful tools.

Where design meets innovation. Feioustudio works at the intersection of design, software, and human-AI interaction, a small practice making a small number of careful things.

The studio

Feioustudio is the independent practice of Feiou Su. The studio's mission is building meaningful interactions between people and the tools they use every day. Its work spans applications, product design, and consumer objects, united by a single principle: that thoughtful tools should make daily life clearer, not noisier.

Feiou's background spans interaction art, AR/VR game design, experience development, and product design. That breadth shapes how Feioustudio approaches software, closer to a craft practice than a feature factory. The best tools, in this view, are the ones you almost stop noticing, because they have already moved out of your way.
