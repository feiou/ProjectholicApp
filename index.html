From intent to action

Plan -> schedule -> finish

Projectholic keeps the sequence simple. Think through the work, place it on the calendar, then finish with clarity. Each step stays lightweight, so you move forward without over-planning.

Plan

Turn your goal into a project. Define milestones, key tasks, and the outcome you want to achieve.

Schedule

Place tasks where they fit. The calendar highlights your true capacity so time stays realistic.

Finish

Track progress over time, adjust with confidence, and close projects with a clear finish line.

Feature Highlights

Project-based calendar

The calendar is organized by project, not chaos. Every task lives under a clear objective so your week stays aligned with what matters.

  • Group tasks by project and milestone
  • See timelines without switching contexts
  • Balance workload across multiple projects

Time is your budget

Projects thrive when you respect time constraints. Budget hours like a resource, then let the schedule reflect reality without guesswork.

  • Allocate time across projects and roles
  • Spot overload before it happens
  • Keep momentum without burnout

Progress you can see

Move beyond checklists. Visual progress shows momentum, reveals patterns, and keeps your attention on finishing.

  • Track velocity and completion trends
  • Spot blockers and refine scope
  • Understand where your time is actually going

Recurring tasks, simplified

Routines keep life stable. Projectholic lets you build recurring tasks that adapt to your schedule without extra effort.

  • Flexible repeat rules
  • Automatic rescheduling
  • Clear history of completions

Share tasks and follow up

Projects move faster when everyone is aligned. Share tasks, assign owners, and keep follow-ups clear with one source of truth.

  • Assign tasks and set expectations
  • Follow up without chasing messages
  • Keep collaboration focused on outcomes

Power view on iPad and Mac

When you need the full picture, open a wide, immersive layout built for deep focus and complex projects.

  • Wide, immersive layout for complex work
  • Built for deep focus and planning
  • Full context across all your projects
