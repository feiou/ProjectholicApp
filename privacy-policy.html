Privacy Policy for Projectholic

Effective Date: September 19, 2025

Feioustudio ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Projectholic app ("the App") handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • No personal information. The App does not collect or store personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or contact details.
  • Anonymous analytics. The App may collect anonymous usage statistics to help us understand how it is used and to improve performance. This information is aggregated and cannot be traced back to you.
  • Subscriptions and purchases. The App manages subscriptions and in-app purchases through Apple's system. To keep your subscription status updated across devices, the App may use a random, anonymous identifier. This identifier cannot be used to personally identify you.

2. Data Storage and Sync

  • iCloud sync. All your project and task data is stored securely in your personal Apple iCloud account. We do not have access to your iCloud data, and it is never transmitted to our servers (we do not operate any servers).

3. How We Use Data

  • To maintain and improve the App's functionality.
  • To process and validate subscriptions.
  • To understand usage trends through anonymous analytics.

4. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (for example, under GDPR in the EU or CCPA in California), you may have the right to request access to or deletion of your data, or to opt out of certain types of data collection (such as analytics).

Since we do not collect personal data, these rights generally do not apply. However, if you have concerns, you may contact us.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the App or on our website.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at feioustudio@gmail.com.
